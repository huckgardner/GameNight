How to Play

1. One player is the Psychic. They see a hidden target zone on a spectrum (like Cold ↔ Hot).

2. The Psychic gives a clue — a word or phrase that fits where the target is. (e.g. target near "hot" → clue: "lava")

3. Everyone else moves the dial to where they think the target is, based only on the clue.

4. The closer to the center of the target, the more points the team scores. Then the role passes to the next Psychic.

The Reveal
How to Play

1. Everyone writes a sentence at the same time.

2. Your sentence passes to the next player, who has to draw it.

3. That drawing passes to the next player, who guesses what it is in words.

4. Write → draw → guess → draw… until it comes back around. Then everyone watches each chain fall apart hilariously.

Best with 4+ players. Works on separate phones.
